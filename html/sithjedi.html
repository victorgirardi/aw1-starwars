Known as protectors of peace, the Jedi are a class of warriors that adhered a doctrine which favored the light side of the Force. The most iconic/powerful Jedi are:

Former apprentice of Dooku and tutor of Obi-Wan Kenobi, Qui-Gon was a Jedi Master who didn't quite followed the Jedi Code as well as the orders of the Jedi Council, in order to follow the will of the Force. In addition, throught his studies of the Living Force, he learnt the secret of eternal consciousness.

Former apprentice of Qui-Gon Jin and mentor of both Anakin Skywalker and Luke Skywalker, Obi-Wan was a Jedi that fought at the Clone Wars (he was made a Jedi Master and member of the Jedi Council at this period) as well as the first Jedi in a millennium to defeat a Sith Lord.

Former apprentice of Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker was a Jedi Knight who fought at the Clone Wars and considered the Chosen One (he would bring balance to the Force). However, at the ending of the Clone Wars, he turned himself to the dark side of the Force, to be known by the name of Darth Vader.

Former apprentice of both Obi-Wan Kenobi and Yoda, Luke Skywalker was a Jedi Knight who fought for the Alliance during the Galatic Civil War. Responsible for the destruction of the first Death Star and for bringing Anakin for the light side of the Force.

Mentor of countless Jedi, Yoda was the wisest Jedi of all time. He serverd as Grand Master of the Jedi Order and also held a seat on the Jedi Council. He was the only Jedi who sensed fear and the danger to the future of the Jedi Order in Anakin Skywalker.

Sith are the enemies of Jedi and use de Dark Side of the Force to improve your power. iconic/powerful Sith are:

Former apprentice of Darth Sidious, Maul or Darth Maul is an assassin who grew in the dark side of the Force and also a skilled lightsaber duelist. Confronted both Qui-Gon Jin and Obi-Wan Kenobi (was only a padawan at the time) during the crisis on Naboo.

Former apprentice of both Yoda and Darth Sidious, Dooku was a Sith Lord who led the Separatists during the Clone Wars. However, he was a Jedi Master once and he left the order due to the corruption in the government. Confronted both Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker (cutting his hand during the fight).

Former apprentice of Darth Plagueis, Sheev Palpatine or Darh Sidious was the Dark Lord of the Sith, responsible for founding the Galatic Empire after destroying the Galatic Republic from inside. He was the Supreme Chancellor of the Republic Galatic before becoming the new Emperor of the Galaxy as well as being the responsible for both beginning and ending of the Clone Wars.

Former apprentice of Darth Sidious, Darth Vader was a Dark Lord of the Sith, guilty in executing the great majority of Jedi latter in the Clone Wars. He was forced to use a life-sustaining suit as the result of confronting Obi-Wan in Mustafar.
