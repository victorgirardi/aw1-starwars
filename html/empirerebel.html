Two humble students of IFSP decided to praise the nitty-gritty of space novels, Star Wars, creating a site where anyone can get some ducky informations about it.

R2-D2 or R2 is the only Jedi droid in the whole franchise. Owing to the fact that R2 often did stuff that other droids couldn't do during both Crisis on Naboo and the Clone Wars, like burning combat droids while waiting for its masters as well as fixing a whole starship at the speed of light.

C-3PO or just 3PO is a unit protocol droid, fluent in over seven million forms of communication. He was built by Anakin Skywalker (during his period as a slave) and aided the both Republic and Rebellion, alongside with R2-D2.

BB-8, just like R2-D2, is a mechanic droid, who belonged to Poe Damaron, whom it accompanied during his flights, and was responsible for keeping the map to find Luke Skywalker safe.

Poe Dameron is a human who served as a pilot in the Resistance (considered the best pilot of it). And he was responsible for the new Saga of films, since he assisted Finn on the escape of the First Order.

Lando Calrissian was smuggler, gambler and the first owner of the Milennium Falcon. During the Galatic Civil War, he was forced by Darth Vader to trick the group of Rebels, including Han Solo and Leia Organa. Aftewards, he joined the Rebellion, helped in the rescue of Han Solo from Jabba The Hutt and also piloted the Millennium Falcon on the destruction of the Second Death Star.

Leia Organa was the pricess of Alderann, leader of the Rebellion and General of the Resistance. She was the person which gave R2-D2 the plans to destroy the First Death Star alongside with the well-known message to Ben Kenobi, at the beginning of the fourth film.

Han Solo or just Han was a smuggler and the best pilot of the galaxy, performing the Kessel run in only 12 parsecs. The infamous pilot of Millennium Falcon, besides Chewbacca, helped both Obi-Wan and Luke to find Leia Organa and ultimately aided Luke on the destruction of the First Death Star. In addition, he engaged on both battles of Hoth and Endor. Latter, he helped the Rebellion on most memorable battles of the Galatic Civil War.

Chewbacca or Chewie is a Wookiee warrior and smuggler who was the co-pilot and closest friend of Han Solo. During the Clone Wars, he aided Master Yoda to fled from the Order 66.
